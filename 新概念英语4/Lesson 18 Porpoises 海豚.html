长期以来，海员中流传着一种迷信的说法，

There has long been a superstition among mariners

认为海豚会把快要淹死的人托到水面，救人性命；

that porpoises will save drowning men by pushing them to the surface,

或在人们周围列队保护，使他们免遭鲨鱼伤害。

or protect them from sharks by surrounding them in defensive formation.

海洋摄影室的生物学家指出，

Marine Studio biologists have pointed out that,

无论海豚多么聪明，

however intelligent they may be,

认为它们有救人的动机可能是错误的。

it is probably a mistake to credit dolphins with any motive of lifesaving.

当它们偶尔把一个失去知觉的人推到岸边时，

On the occasions when they have pushed to shore an unconscious human being

更大的可能是出于好奇或游戏，

they have much more likely done it out of curiosity or for sport,

就像它们追逐被船首犁开的浪花一样。

as in riding the bow waves of a ship.

1928年，有人拍摄到了海豚像海狸一样把浸透水的床垫推上岸的情景。

In 1928 some porpoises were photographer working like beavers to push ashore a waterlogged mattress.

正如报道中所说，如果海豚保护人不受鲨鱼侵害，

If, as has been reported, they have protected humans from sharks,

那么它们可能是出于好奇；而鲨鱼可能是闻到了可以美食一顿的香味。

it may have been because curiosity attracted them and because the scent of a possible meal attracted the sharks.

海豚和鲨鱼是天然仇敌，

Porpoises and sharks are natural enemies.

双方可能随之发生搏斗，

It is possible that upon such an occasion a battle ensued,

搏斗结果是海豚赶走或咬死鲨鱼。

with the sharks being driven away or killed.

海豚对凡是活的东西都感兴趣，不管是鸟、是鱼，还是野兽。

Whether it be bird, fish or beast, the porpoise is intrigued with anything that is alive.

它们经常追逐海龟，

They are constantly after the turtles,

海龟则温顺地忍受着各种侮辱。

who peacefully submit to all sorts of indignities.

一只小海豚特别喜欢用鼻子把海龟推到水面，

One young calf especially enjoyed raising a turtle to the surface with his snout

然后像滑水板一样把海龟从水池的这一边推到那一边。

and then shoving him across the tank like an aquaplane.

几乎每天都可以看到一只小海豚把鼻子顶入一只300磅重的海龟的硬壳下面，拼命地把它翻过来。

Almost any day a young porpoise may be seen trying to turn a 300-pound sea turtle over by sticking his snout under the edge of his shell and pushing up for dear life.

这并非易事，可能需要两只海豚合伙干才行。

This is not easy, and may require two porpoises working together.

在另一场游戏中，当海龟游过水族馆时，

In another game, as the turtle swims across the oceanarium,

第一只海豚从上方猛扑下去，用腹部撞击龟壳。

the first porpoise swoops down from above and butts his shell with his belly.

这一下子把海龟撞下去好几英尺。

This knocks the turtle down several feet.

海龟刚恢复平衡，第二只海豚又冲过来猛击一下。

He no sooner recovers his equilibrium than the next porpoise comes along and hits him another crack.

这只海龟最终被撞到池底。

Eventually the turtle has been butted all the way down to the floor of the tank.

此时的海龟，只要能站起来就满足了，

He is now satisfied merely to try to stand up,

但它刚站起来，就被一只海豚击倒。

but as soon as he does so a porpoise knocks him flat.

海龟终于屈服了，将4条腿缩进壳内。游戏到此结束。

The turtle at last gives up by pulling his feet under his shell and the game is over.
